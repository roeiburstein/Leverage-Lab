📊 Strategy Mechanics Comparison

🛡️ Buffer Bands (Hysteresis)
Standard SMA Trend Binary. Triggers allocations immediately when price crosses an SMA line. Leads to massive "whipsaw" friction losses around flat moving averages.
Enhanced Quant SMA Asymmetric Buffers. Requires price to exceed SMA by +3% (on 200 SMA) or +2% (on 50 SMA) to escalate leverage, and fall below by -1.5% or -1% to exit.
